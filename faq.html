What is Temp Number and how does it work?

Temp Number is a free online service that provides temporary, disposable phone numbers for receiving SMS messages. You can use these virtual numbers to verify accounts on platforms like WhatsApp, Telegram, Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, and hundreds of other services — all without revealing your personal phone number.

Simply choose a country, pick a number, and start receiving verification codes and messages instantly. No registration or sign-up is required.

How is this service free of charge?

We provide our temporary phone number service at no cost to our users. Our revenue comes from advertising displayed on the website, allowing us to keep the service 100% free. If you do not see any ads, please consider disabling your ad-blocker to support our service. There are no hidden fees, premium subscriptions, or in-app purchases required.

What does the number in parentheses beside a phone number represent?

The number in parentheses represents the total count of SMS messages that have been processed by that particular phone number since it was added. Please note that not all of these messages may be displayed, as older messages are automatically removed after approximately 7 days to maintain storage efficiency.

Can anyone access the contents of my messages?

Yes, the incoming messages to our temporary phone numbers are visible to anyone who views that number's inbox page. These are shared, public numbers. However, we block out part of the sender's information to enhance privacy.

We strongly recommend not using these numbers for sensitive personal communications, banking verifications, or any accounts tied to your real identity. Use them for testing, secondary accounts, or one-time verifications.

How long are messages stored on your servers?

Messages are typically kept for around 7 days after they are first received. After that period, they are automatically purged from our system. This ensures privacy and keeps our infrastructure running efficiently. If you need a message, make sure to read or copy it within the 7-day window.
