What Is Temp Number?

Everything you need to know about our temporary phone number service.

Temp Number is a free online platform that provides temporary, disposable phone numbers for SMS verification purposes. When you sign up for a website or app that requires a phone number, you can use one of our virtual numbers instead of your personal one, protecting your real identity and inbox from spam.

We offer over 7,000+ free numbers from 50+ countries worldwide, including the United States, United Kingdom, Canada, Germany, France, Netherlands, India, and many more. Each number receives real SMS messages that appear in a shared public inbox, accessible to anyone, making the service completely transparent and free.

Whether you need to verify a social media account, sign up for an online service, test an application, or simply avoid sharing your personal number, Temp Number has you covered. No registration is required for most countries, and the entire service is completely free of charge.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about Temp Number.

Is Temp Number really free? ▾
Yes! Our service is 100% free. We're supported by advertising, which allows us to keep the service running without charging users. There are no hidden fees, subscriptions, or premium paywalls for basic use.
Do I need to create an account? ▾
No registration is needed for most countries. Simply browse our numbers, pick one, and start receiving SMS. Some numbers from regulated countries may require a free account for compliance purposes.
Can others see my messages? ▾
Yes, our free numbers have shared, public inboxes. This means anyone viewing that number can see all incoming messages. We recommend not using these numbers for sensitive personal accounts like banking or email recovery.
How long do numbers stay active? ▾
Numbers stay active as long as they continue receiving messages. Inactive numbers may be retired, but we constantly add new ones. Our system is designed to always have thousands of working numbers available, currently over 7,000.
Is there a mobile app? ▾
Yes! Temp Number is available as a native app on both iOS (App Store) and Android (Google Play). The app offers push notifications, faster access, private numbers, and a smoother mobile experience.
Which services work with your numbers? ▾
Our numbers work with most major services including Google, Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, Telegram, Twitter/X, Amazon, PayPal, Uber, and thousands more. Some services may block disposable numbers. If one doesn't work, simply try another.
