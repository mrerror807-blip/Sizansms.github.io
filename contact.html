Frequently Asked Questions

I didn't receive the verification SMS — what should I do? ▾
SMS can take 30–60 seconds to arrive. Refresh the inbox page and wait. If it still doesn't appear, the service you're verifying with may have blocked public numbers. Try a different number from the same or another country, or consider using a premium private number for guaranteed delivery.
Are free numbers shared with other users? ▾
Yes — free numbers are public and shared among all users. Messages are visible to everyone. For private, dedicated numbers that only you can access, check out our premium plans.
How do I request a refund for a premium number? ▾
If you purchased a premium number and didn't receive the expected SMS within the guaranteed window, contact us at [email protected] with your order details. We'll review and process eligible refunds within 48 hours.
Can I use Temp Number for illegal activities? ▾
Absolutely not. Temp Number is designed for legitimate privacy protection — like signing up for services without exposing your personal phone number. Any misuse violates our Terms of Service and may result in immediate account suspension.
Do you offer business or bulk plans? ▾
Yes — if you need numbers in bulk for testing, QA, or business workflows, reach out to [email protected] with your requirements and we'll provide a custom quote.
Which countries do you support? ▾
